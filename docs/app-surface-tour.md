# App Surface Tour

This document explains the app from the user point of view rather than from an internal codebase point of view.

## Core surfaces

### Home

Home introduces the product direction and gives the user a controlled entry into the important routes.

### AI Chat

AI Chat is the conversational entry for:

- help
- route guidance
- quick explanations
- approval-first task routing

### AI Cockpit

AI Cockpit is the operator workspace. It is where the user should expect guided movement between:

- coding
- creator workflows
- browser-assisted tasks
- future automation

### Vault

Vault exists to preserve user trust. It is the place where local-first ownership, profile state, recovery direction, and safe evidence handling are centered.

### Market

Market must never feel empty. The launch direction is a first-visit starter drop plus clear explanation of the catalog and proof-gated paid flows.

### Realm / EON City

Realm is the flagship experiential route. It is part world, part product map, part workstation gateway.

### Telegram / Reward Center

Telegram is a growth and reward gateway, but it must remain explicit:

- user tap required
- no automatic ad launch
- verified reward truth for real entitlements

## Why this matters

A public showcase that only shows code snippets is not enough. People need to understand the product shape. This document exists to give that bigger picture without revealing private implementation details.
