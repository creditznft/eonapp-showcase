# Reward Safety Model

## Rewarded ads

Verified rewarded ads are the real account-wide reward path. They require provider proof and/or postback verification.

## Direct Link

Direct Link must be user-tap only. It can provide a local soft Sponsor Boost but must not unlock paid access alone.

## MultiTag

MultiTag is off by default. A user can opt into extra Sponsor Boost earning, and they can turn it off again.

## Blocked routes

Sponsor Boost should stay blocked on sensitive routes such as billing, legal, privacy, Vault secrets, support, admin, backup, and payment pages.
